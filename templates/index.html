Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?
Our AI model has been trained on extensive educational data and achieves an accuracy rate of over 90% in most cases. However, predictions should be used as a guide rather than an absolute measure.
What factors are considered in the predictions?
We consider various factors including past academic performance, attendance rates, study habits, extracurricular activities, and socio-economic indicators to provide a comprehensive prediction.
How can I improve a student's predicted performance?
Our system provides personalized recommendations based on the prediction results. These may include targeted tutoring, study skill improvements, or addressing specific areas of weakness.
Is my students' data kept confidential?
Absolutely. We take data privacy very seriously. All student data is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with all relevant data protection regulations.
